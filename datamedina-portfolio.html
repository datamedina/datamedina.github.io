Statistical Min/Max Inventory Model

The client's POS system only exported daily snapshots — not time-series reports. With no date column in raw exports, calculating reorder points was impossible. I built a pipeline that consolidates 27+ daily CSV files into a single BigQuery table, parses dates from filenames, and computes per-product safety stock at a ~95% service level using a z-score of 1.65.
